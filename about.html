Quarantinediary.ai is a diary that's jointly shaped by the artist, an LSTR machine learning algorithm trained by news articles published during COVID19.

In the Buddhist metaphor of Indra’s Net, it is stated that “In the heaven of Indra, there is said to be an infinite network of pearls, so arranged that if you look at one you see all the others reflected in it.” Quarantinediary.ai documents how thoughts of fear and unsettlement are felt upon and recipriocated through the internet during the global pandamic.


Thanks Pierre Marzin, Daniel Shiffman and the p5.js Foundation for supplying some opensource javascript code examples that was used to build visual effects.

Thanks Frank-Ratchye STUDIO for Creative Inquiry at Carnegie Mellon University for sponsoring the LSTM neural network research that was used to generate the texts.
